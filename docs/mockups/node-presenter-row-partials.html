Resolver-provided surface

  • Primary label and href used by the row partial.
  • Tooltip or title text for presenter-owned links.
  • Badge, icon, and optional action metadata that remains product-neutral here.

Host-app-owned surface

  • Business columns, counts, routes, and authorization policy.
  • Final action behavior, disabled reasons, and workflow copy.
  • Any Column or Action DSL design beyond the row partial cookbook.
