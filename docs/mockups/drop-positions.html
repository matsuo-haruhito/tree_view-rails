Disabled and responsibility boundary

TreeView can show a coarse target cue, but it does not decide whether a move is allowed for the host application's business rules.

  • Use data-tree-drop-position for visual comparison of before, inside, and after positions.
  • Use disabled or invalid styling only as representative host-app policy output in this static reference.
  • Keep final permission wording, persistence, audit trails, and reorder rules outside this mockup.
