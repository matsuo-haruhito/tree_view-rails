State cues to review

Variant What it shows What stays out of scope
Enabled pair Both actions available while the tree contains a mix of expanded and collapsed branches. Route names, Turbo wiring, or action sequencing rules.
Expand disabled The tree is already fully expanded, so only collapse remains actionable. Business copy about why a branch is open or who may collapse it.
Collapse disabled The tree is already fully collapsed, so only expand remains actionable. Authorization, saved preferences, or persistence across visits.
