Review cues

  • Expanded rows should communicate the current path, not a full sidebar product policy.
  • Collapsed sibling branches should keep toggle, depth, and hidden-count cues visible without exposing their children.
  • Breadcrumb labels and current-row labels may repeat, but their responsibilities stay separate: path context outside the table, hierarchy state inside the table.
  • Current-row copy, routes, permissions, and final sidebar behavior remain host-app responsibilities.
