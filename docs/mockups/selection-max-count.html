What this mock compares

Surface Best for Keep outside scope
Selected count Showing the current count and max near the tree in a product-neutral way. Final localized copy, action availability, and bulk action policy.
Limit reached Making the max-count state visible without turning every unchecked row into a permission problem. Authorization, disabled business controls, and route-specific submit behavior.
